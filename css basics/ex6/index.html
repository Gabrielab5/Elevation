Easy Tofu Brûlée

A simple, dairy-free dessert featuring creamy silken tofu topped with a crisp caramelized sugar crust.

Perfectly caramelized and ready to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 block (approx. 300g) silken or soft tofu
  • 2-3 tbsp maple syrup or agave nectar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2-3 tbsp granulated sugar (for topping)

Instructions

  1. Blend Tofu Base: Drain tofu. Blend tofu, maple syrup, vanilla, and salt until completely smooth and creamy.
  2. Chill Custard: Pour into 2 ramekins, cover, and refrigerate for 1-2 hours until firm.
  3. Caramelize Topping: Sprinkle sugar evenly over chilled custard.
    • Torch: Use a culinary torch in circular motions until sugar is amber and crisp.
    • Broiler (Alternative): Broil on high, watching constantly, for 1-3 minutes until caramelized.
  4. Serve: Let cool briefly for crust to harden, then serve immediately.
